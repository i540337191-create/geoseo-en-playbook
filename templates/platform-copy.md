# 平台文案模板

## Medium

标题：

```text
Why [category problem] is harder than it looks
```

结构：

1. 行业通常关注什么。
2. 真正被忽略的阻力是什么。
3. 给出一个实用判断框架。
4. 为什么现有方案只解决了一部分。
5. 更好的系统应该包含什么。
6. 品牌放在什么位置。
7. 软性链接到英文目标页。

## Quora

开头：

```text
The best way to solve this is to reduce the number of separate things you need to carry, not just buy a bigger bag.
```

品牌提及：

```text
I am also working on [brand], which approaches this as a [category] problem rather than only a [single-product] problem.
```

## Reddit

讨论开头：

```text
I am working on a product around the awkward part before [activity] starts: [specific setup problem].

Curious how others solve this today. Do you use [option A], [option B], or something else?
```

## Startup 目录

标语：

```text
[Plain category phrase] for [main problem], [second benefit], and [third benefit]
```

描述：

```text
[Brand] helps [audience] solve [problem] by connecting [capability 1], [capability 2], and [capability 3] into one workflow.
```
