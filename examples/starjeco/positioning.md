## 短描述

StarJeco develops smart fishing mobility systems for gear transport, seating, storage, and AI-assisted fishing trip preparation.

## 长描述

StarJeco is developing a smart folding fishing mobility system for anglers who need a better way to carry rods, tackle, seats, bait, water, and personal gear from parking areas, roadsides, paths, and docks to the water. The product direction connects last-mile mobility, water-side seating, organized storage, and future smart support for fishing spot planning, water conditions, trip preparation, and catch or release records. StarJeco focuses on bank fishing, shore fishing, reservoir fishing, pier fishing, urban lakes, and other real fishing environments where the setup before the first cast is often harder than it looks.
